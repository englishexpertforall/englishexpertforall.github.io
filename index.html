About Me

I am a highly experienced English teacher from Canada with 14+ years of teaching experience. I understand all sorts of accents and am familiar with English vocabulary from different countries. My expertise ranges from beginners to highly advanced grammar.

My teaching journey includes:

  • 7 years teaching in Canada
  • 7 years teaching in Moscow and other cities in Russia
  • Experience with all age categories, from toddlers to adults
  • Teaching in schools, private residences, and as a complete day tutor
  • Accompanying families on trips and caring for children
  • Extensive experience with online teaching platforms

I am highly patient, creative, and energetic, explaining everything in detail. I have experience teaching children with ADHD and adapt my methods to each student's needs. I'm also sportive, having played soccer professionally, which helps me connect with students through various interests.

With consistent classes, you'll see noticeable results within weeks. I offer flexible scheduling for online classes or in-person lessons at your location or mine.
